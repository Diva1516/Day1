TOP 5 Most Watched Indian film Traileres on Youtube

1.KGF- 280 million

K.G.F: Chapter 2 is a 2022 Indian Kannada-language action drama film written and directed by Prashanth Neel and produced by Hombale Films. Serving as the sequel to K.G.F: Chapter 1 (2018), it continues the saga of Rocky Bhai’s rise to power in the violent gold-mining underworld of Kolar Gold Fields. Released globally on April 14 2022, it became one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.

2.Salaar- 150 million

Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by Prashanth Neel and produced by Hombale Films. Starring Prabhas and Prithviraj Sukumaran, it unfolds in the violent, fictional kingdom of Khansaar, exploring friendship, loyalty, and betrayal through epic set-piece battles and stylized world-building. The film became one of the highest-grossing Indian releases of 2023 and a major streaming success.

3.War - 142 million

War is a 2019 Indian Hindi-language action thriller directed by Siddharth Anand and produced by Yash Raj Films. The film stars Hrithik Roshan and Tiger Shroff as intelligence agents whose mentor–student relationship unravels when one goes rogue. It became one of the highest-grossing Hindi films of 2019, praised for its stylized action and star power.

4.Baaghi 3 - 134 million

Baaghi 3 is a 2020 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Ahmed Khan and produced by Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment and Fox Star Studios. It is the third installment in the Baaghi film series, starring Tiger Shroff, Shraddha Kapoor, and Riteish Deshmukh. The movie is known for its high-octane action sequences and large-scale production.

5.Zero - 125 million

Zero is a 2018 Indian Hindi-language romantic comedy-drama directed by Aanand L. Rai. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan as a vertically challenged man navigating love and identity, alongside Anushka Sharma and Katrina Kaif. Noted for its ambitious visual effects and emotional scope, Zero explores themes of self-acceptance and purpose through an unconventional romantic journey.
